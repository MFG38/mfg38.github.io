Title                   : Bleeding Skies
Filename                : bskies.pk3
Author                  : Santtu "MFG38" Pesonen
Email Address           : mfg38thelps@aol.com

Description             : Bleeding Skies is a Doom 2 wad created by MFG38,
                          powered by the GZDoom engine. You assume the role
                          of Flynn Carmack, an army veteran who recently
			  lost his wife to the aliens that have invaded
                          Earth and seeks revenge. But he is also
                          mankind's last chance to win the war that
                          rages...

Additional Credits to   : id Software, Randy Heit, Graf Zahl, MrVonsta,
                          Magnificent Beard, and Paul Smart at Piece of Pie
                          Software, as well as the respective authors of
			  resources used in the wad.
