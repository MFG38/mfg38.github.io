Title                   : 84cy-XII
Filename                : 84cy-xii.wad
Release date            : 2021-10-11
Author                  : Santtu "MFG38" Pesonen
Email Address           : mfg38thelps@aol.com

Description             : 84cy-XII is a small puzzle map consisting of a
                          single room. It has a window. It has a lamp. It has
                          a bed. It has a bookcase. It has a box. It has a
                          door. Work your brain cells and make your escape.
                          
                          Made as a speedmap in 45 minutes. Features a custom
                          animated sky made by Yours Truly, other than that,
                          the map uses vanilla textures.

Additional Credits to   : Authors of MIDIs used:
                          foxT.A.K.A (title, "Mansion (2nd Floor)" from
                          Resident Evil)
                          Michael Barrette (MAP01, "Watch Your Step" from
                          Doom 64)
