* Construction *

Base                    : Doom PLAYPAL/COLORMAP, quantized to 32 colors and
                          modified by hand where seen fit
Build Time              : A couple of hours (most of which was testing the
                          visuals and tweaking the colormap) plus some weeks
                          of procrastinating on releasing the final version
Editor(s) used          : SLADE3, Paint.net, DoomCrap palette dumper
May Not Run With        : Should work in anything except maybe the original
                          executable.
Tested With             : Chocolate Doom 3.0.0, Crispy Doom 6.0.0, dsda-doom
                          0.25.6
