Qwirk

Status Finished
Year 2021
Genre Platformer
Engine Clickteam Fusion 2.5

DOWNLOAD LINKS

Qwirk is a small proof-of-concept Metroidvania where the extra abilties are temporary instead of permanent. Upon finding a powerup, it’s added to your “spell pool”, and you can cast it to use it for a short time. The game also has collectibles that are scattered around the game world, but they’re merely a completion incentive.
