Happy Red Ball Thing

Status Finished
Year 2017
Genre Platformer
Engine Clickteam Fusion 2.5

DOWNLOAD LINKS

Happy Red Ball Thing is an incredibly small, simplistic 2D platformer. The objective in this game is to guide a red ball through 12 levels. The goal is marked by a red pole, and the player has to reach it while avoiding yellow blocks (and falling out of the level). The levels also have par times for those who want to complete them as fast as possible.
