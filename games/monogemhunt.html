MonoGemHunt

Status Finished
Year 2020
Genre Adventure
Engine Clickteam Fusion 2.5

DOWNLOAD LINKS

MonoGemHunt is a minimalistic 2D top-down shooter starring a blue cube. The main objective of MonoGemHunt revolves around hunting for a key in order to unlock the exit. The game also has collectibles, usually hidden behind destructible blocks that the player can shoot. The collectibles aren’t really essential in beating the game, though – they’re merely there as a completion incentive.
