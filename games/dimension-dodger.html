Dimension Dodger

Status Finished
Year 2021
Genre Arcade
Engine Clickteam Fusion 2.5

DOWNLOAD LINKS

Dimension Dodger is a simple arcade-style game where the objective is to switch the color of your spaceship according to the color of the projectile flying at you and survive as long as possible. The game was made for the Boundless Game Jam, where the theme was “one button”.
