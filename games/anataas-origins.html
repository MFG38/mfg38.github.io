Anataas: Origins is a spin-off game in the MFG38's Average Adventure storyline, focusing on the main antagonist Anataas. The player assumes his role as he looks for a place he can call home but gets caught up in something much bigger than his personal struggles. Was he always a dick? What's his relation to the goddess of darkness? What was up with that one conversation towards the end of MFG38's Average Adventure 2? These are the questions that this game aims to answer.
