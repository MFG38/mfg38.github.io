moonbound is a small, casual Metroidvania-style game. You control a little dude(tte) whose rocket has crash-landed on an alien planet, with a tree between you and your means of escape. With that, you must delve into the caves below and find enough jump powerups to be able to get back to your rocket.

The game was made for Metroidvania Month 19.
